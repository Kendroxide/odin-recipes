Pancakes

Description

Enjoy these healthy and fluffy pancakes anytime! Easy to make and more nutritional compared to most other pancakes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup white flour
  • 2 tablespoons chia seeds(optional)
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar or honey
  • 1 1/2 cup milk (whole milk or any type preferred)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla
  • Half cup plain greek yoghurt

Instructions

  1. Mix together the flour, chia seeds, baking powder, sugar/honey in a large bowl
  2. In a seperate bowl, beat two eggs. Once eggs are beaten, add the milk, vanilla, and greek yoghurt. Mix with a whisk until an even consistency.
  3. Combine the wet ingredients with the dry ingredients. Stir until an even consistency. Make sure to not over stir.
  4. Let the batter sit for 10 minutes, or longer.
  5. Heat a frying pan on medium heat and lightly coat with oil
  6. Cook each side until a golden brown. Then enjoy with honey or fruit!
