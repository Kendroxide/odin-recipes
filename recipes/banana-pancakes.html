Banana Pancakes

These are easy to make and a great way to use up old bananas. By adding protein powder, these pack a nutritionally dense morning breakfast and are quick to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 large or 3 small bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • Half cup protein poweder (can substitute white flour)
  • Half cup rolled oats
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • Splash of vanilla
  • Teaspoon of cinnamon poweder

Instructions

  1. Mash bananas with a fork until a smooth consistency.
  2. Mix in eggs and beat together
  3. Pour in butter slowely while mixing. This ensures that the hot butter does not cook the eggs. Butter should also be allowed to cool slightly if hot.
  4. Add the remaining ingredients and whisk together
  5. Allow the batter to rest for at least 10 minutes. This will allow the rolled oats to absorb some of the moister and creates a better consistency.
  6. Cook on a lightly oiled pan at low heat
  7. I like to top the pancakes with peanut butter. Feel free to use nutella, syrup, honey or fresh fruit instead.
